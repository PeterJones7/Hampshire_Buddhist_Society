The object of the Hampshire Buddhist Society is to make known the principles of Buddhism and to encourage the study and practice of those principles. We comprise both a Theravada and a Zen group.

Theravada Group Zen Group

In addition to their weekly Zoom meetings, the Theravada group arranges an in-person silent retreat in Romsey on the second Saturday of every month.

The Society does not charge for meetings and relies on donations to cover its costs.
